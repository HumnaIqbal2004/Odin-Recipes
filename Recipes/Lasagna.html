Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is one of those dishes that feels like a warm hug straight from the oven. Layers of tender pasta, rich and savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and perfectly melted cheese come together to create something truly comforting. It’s hearty, satisfying, and always worth the little bit of extra time it takes to assemble.

This classic homemade lasagna is perfect for family dinners, gatherings, or whenever you’re craving real comfort food. The layers bake together into a bubbling, golden masterpiece that slices beautifully and tastes even better the next day.

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 jar (24 oz / 700g) marinara sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Cheese Layer:

  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese (divided)
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley (or 1 teaspoon dried)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the meat sauce, In a large pan over medium heat, cook the ground beef until browned. Add chopped onion and cook until softened. Stir in garlic and cook for about 30 seconds. Add marinara sauce, tomato paste, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10–15 minutes to let the flavors develop.
  2. Make the cheese mixture, In a bowl, combine ricotta, egg, Parmesan, parsley, and 1 cup of mozzarella. Mix until smooth and well combined.
  3. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  4. Layer the Lasagna
    • Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of the dish.
    • Add a layer of lasagna noodles.
    • Spread some of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
    • Add a layer of meat sauce.
    • Repeat the layers (usually 3 layers total).
    • Finish with meat sauce and the remaining 1 cup mozzarella on top.
  5. Bake, Cover with foil (tent it slightly so it doesn’t stick to the cheese) and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake another 20–25 minutes until bubbly and golden on top.
  6. Rest before serving, Let the lasagna rest for 10–15 minutes before slicing. This helps the layers set and makes serving easier.
