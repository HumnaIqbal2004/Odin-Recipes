MeatLoaf

Description

There’s something comforting about a good old-fashioned meatloaf. It’s the kind of meal that fills the kitchen with rich, savory smells and brings everyone to the table without having to call twice. Tender, juicy, and perfectly seasoned, this classic meatloaf is topped with a slightly sweet and tangy glaze that caramelizes beautifully in the oven.

Whether you’re serving it with creamy mashed potatoes, roasted vegetables, or saving leftovers for the best meatloaf sandwich the next day, this recipe is simple, reliable, and full of homemade flavor. No fancy ingredients — just hearty, honest comfort food.

Ingredients

For the Meatloaf:

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef (80/20 for best flavor)
  • 1 small onion(finely chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves(minced)
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley (or italian seasoning)

For the Glaze

  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1-2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon mustard(yellow or dijon)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Lightly grease a loaf pan or line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Mix the meatloaf base, In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, chopped onion, garlic, breadcrumbs, egg, milk, ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, salt, pepper, and parsley. Mix gently with your hands until just combined — don’t overwork it, or the meatloaf can turn dense.
  3. Shape the loafTransfer the mixture into your loaf pan or shape it into a loaf on the baking sheet. Smooth the top evenly.
  4. Make the glaze, In a small bowl, mix ketchup, brown sugar, and mustard. Spread half of the glaze over the meatloaf.
  5. Bake Bake for 40–50 minutes. Remove from the oven, spread the remaining glaze on top, and return to the oven for another 10–15 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 70°C (160°F).
  6. Rest before slicing, Let the meatloaf rest for about 10 minutes before slicing. This helps keep it juicy and makes it easier to cut clean slices.
